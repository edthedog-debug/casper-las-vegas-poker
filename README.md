# 🃏 Casper Neon Casino - Las Vegas Texas Hold'em 👻

Welcome to **Casper Neon Casino**, a vibrant, fast-paced web-based Texas Hold'em poker game set against a high-energy Las Vegas neon backdrop. Play against 4 unique AI ghost opponents and watch your winning probabilities shift in real-time with every community card dealt!

🚀 **Live Demo:** [Play Casper Neon Poker Here!](https://edthedog-debug.github.io/casper-las-vegas-poker/)

## 🌟 Key Features

* **Vegas Neon Aesthetic:** A dark casino felt table featuring glowing pink, gold, and cyan neon animations.
* **Ghostly Card Themes:** Custom-styled card backs featuring a hovering, glowing Casper-inspired ghost (`👻`) animation.
* **Real-Time Heuristic Odds Calculator:** Calculates and displays your estimated win probability at every stage of the hand (Preflop, Flop, Turn, and River).
* **Smooth Animations:** Immersive card dealing physics and glowing visual cues to highlight active player turns and winners.
* **GitHub Pages Ready:** Built entirely using vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No installations, server setups, or complex builds required.

## 📈 Live Odds Mechanics

The built-in poker equity engine monitors your hole cards along with the community cards on the fly. It weights your hand strengths based on:
* Pocket pairs (huge pre-flop boost)
* High card values (Aces, Kings, Queens, Jacks)
* Suited connectors
* Algorithmic variations adjusting dynamically through the **Flop**, **Turn**, and **River** stages.
